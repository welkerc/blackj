📖 Klondike Rules

Objective

Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, built up by suit from Ace to King.

Setup

7 tableau columns. Column 1 has 1 card, column 2 has 2 cards, etc. Top card of each column is face-up.

Rules

  • Tableau: Build down in alternating colors (red on black, black on red)
  • Foundations: Build up by suit (Ace, 2, 3... King)
  • Stock: Click to draw cards to waste pile
  • Empty columns: Only Kings can be placed
  • Draw 1: Draw one card at a time (easier)
  • Draw 3: Draw three cards at a time (harder, but more options)

How to Play

Click a face-up card to select it, then click where you want to move it. Double-click to auto-move to foundation if possible.
