Game Rules

Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

  • Card Values: Number cards = face value, Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10, Ace = 1 or 11
  • Hit: Draw another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet, get one more card, then stand
  • Split: If you have two cards of the same value, split into two hands
  • Blackjack: Ace + 10/J/Q/K on first two cards = 3:2 payout
  • Dealer: Must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17+
  • Bust: Going over 21 = lose

Betting: $1 - $100 per hand | Starting bankroll: $1,000

Objective: Win all 52 cards by having the higher card.

  • Gameplay: Each player flips the top card of their deck
  • Higher Card Wins: The player with the higher card takes both
  • Tie = War: When cards match, each player puts 3 cards face-down + 1 face-up
  • War Winner: The player with the higher face-up card wins all cards in the war
  • Double War: If there's another tie during war, the war continues
  • Game End: One player has all cards, or a player runs out

Tip: Wars can accumulate quickly - keep track of the pile!

Objective: Be the first to play all 20 cards.

  • Setup: Each player gets 20 cards (4 face-down, 4 face-up, 12 in hand)
  • Card Values: Ace=1, 2-10=face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13 (King is high)
  • Play Rules: Play cards of equal or LESSER value than the pile
  • Clear the Deck: Play 4+ cards of the same value to clear the pile + get another turn
  • Higher Value: Playing higher value forces you to take the pile + get another turn
  • Wild Tens: Tens can clear the deck at any time, but count as 25 points if held at end
  • Scoring: Cards remaining = points (10s = 25 pts). Lowest score wins!

Tip: Save those tens until you need them!
