🚀 Clear the Deck Rules

Objective: Be the first to play all 20 cards.

Rules adapted from clear-the-deck.com

Setup

  • Each player gets 20 cards:
    • 4 face-down cards
    • 4 face-up cards (on top of face-down)
    • 12 cards in hand
  • One card is placed face-up to start the pile

Card Values

  • Ace = 1 (low), 2-10 = face value, J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13 (high)

Playing Cards

  • You must play cards of equal or LESSER value than the pile
  • You can play multiple cards of the same value at once
  • Face-down cards can only be played once their face-up cards are gone

Special Moves

  • Clear the Deck: Play 4+ cards of the same value to clear the pile + take another turn
  • Higher Value: Playing higher value forces you to take the pile + take another turn
  • Wild Tens: Tens can clear the deck at any time, but count as 25 points if held at the end

Scoring

  • First player out = 0 points
  • Other players score points for remaining cards
  • Numbered cards = face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13, Tens=25
  • Lowest score wins!
