⚔️ War Rules

Objective: Win all 52 cards by having the higher card.

How to Play

  • Each player flips the top card of their deck
  • The player with the higher card takes both cards
  • Cards are added to the bottom of the winner's deck

War

  • When both players flip cards of the same value, it's War!
  • Each player puts 3 cards face-down + 1 card face-up
  • The player with the higher face-up card wins all cards
  • If there's another tie, the war continues!

Game End

  • One player has all 52 cards
  • Or a player runs out of cards during war

Tips

  • Wars can accumulate quickly
  • Keep track of the pile during long wars
  • Patience is key - luck plays a big role!
