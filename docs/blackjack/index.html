🃏 Blackjack Rules

Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards = face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (you choose)

Actions

  • Hit: Draw another card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Double Down: Double your bet, get one more card, then stand
  • Split: Split pairs into two separate hands

Game Rules

  • Blackjack: Ace + 10/J/Q/K on first two cards = 3:2 payout
  • Dealer: Must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17+
  • Bust: Going over 21 = automatic loss
