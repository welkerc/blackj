🎲 Zilch Rules

Objective: Be the first to reach 10,000 points!

How to Play

  • Roll 6 dice
  • Select scoring dice to "freeze"
  • Bank your points or roll remaining dice
  • If you roll no scoring dice = ZILCH! (lose all turn points)
  • Score all 6 dice = roll all 6 again!

Scoring

  • 1 = 100 points
  • 5 = 50 points
  • Three of a kind = Number × 100
  • Three 1s = 1000 points
  • Three pairs = 1000 points
  • Straight (1-2-3-4-5-6) = 1000 points

Strategy Tips

  • Higher odds of zilching with fewer dice
  • Consider banking when you have a good score
  • Scoring all 6 dice gives you another roll!
